📡 Connect your Google Sheet — One-time setup (2 minutes)

  1. Open your Google Sheet
  2. Click File in the top menu → Share → Publish to web
  3. In the first dropdown select the tab "main sheet"
  4. In the second dropdown select Comma-separated values (.csv)
  5. Click the Publish button → click OK
  6. Copy the link that appears (starts with https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/…/pub?…)
  7. Paste it below and click Connect ✅
